3.2 Some Geometries Are Easier To Understand Than Others

“Ordering elementary tasks by accuracy (Cleveland and McGill 1985):”

  1. Position along a common scale

  3. Length
  4. Angle & Slope
  5. Area
  6. Volume, Density, Color Saturation
  7. Color Hue
